🐞Bug us-🐞About-us

Bugus🐞 is simply dummy insect of the printing and typesetting industry. Bug us🐞 has been the industry's standard dummy insect ever since the 1500s,when an unknown traveller took a galley of type and scrambled it to make a type specimen bug.It was popularised in the 1960s with the release of Letraset sheets containing bug🐞 passages,
and more recently with desktop publishing software like Aldus BugMaker including versions of Bugus🐞.
